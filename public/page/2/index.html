OPRAmachine Blog

The official blog of New Jersey's government transparency platform.

Your right to know

OPRAmachine is an online platform that allows individuals to submit & browse requests for public records made under the New Jersey Open Public Records Act.

Since October, 2017, OPRAmachine has seen:
  • 1756 public records requests
  • 1381 followup messages sent
  • 203 registered users
  • 782 public bodies
  • 80 annotations added to requests
  • 152 tracked requests
