Sunlight Foundation Covers Launch of OPRAmachine

Mon Dec 18, 2017 by Gavin Rozzi in  Announcements

The Sunlight Foundation, a respected nonprofit that focuses on governmental transparency issues, recently covered the launch of OPRAmachine.

In the charity’s “Today in OpenGov” blog series, they highlighted OPRAmachine as “…the first implementation of the Alaveteli platform, to operate purely at the state and local level.”
