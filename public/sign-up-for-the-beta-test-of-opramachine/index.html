Sign up for the beta test of OPRAmachine

Sun May 28, 2017 by Gavin Rozzi in  Uncategorized

NOTE: It is no longer necessary to sign up for the beta. You may register for an account on the public OPRAmachine.com website now.

If you are interested in beta testing New Jersey’s first online government transparency platform, please complete the following form. We will be accepting applications for beta testers, and when the site is ready for testing, a closed beta will take place to collect feedback before launching the site for the general public.

You can access the form directly on this page below. Be sure to scroll all the way down and click “submit” when you have finished filling it out with your information.
